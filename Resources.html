Tutorials

Guides to learning GW-BASIC or other forms of BASIC.

  • The Secret Guide to Computers - Other Basics: Long-scrolling page quickly describing different versions of BASIC, including GW-BASIC and Visual Basic for DOS. As educational information it is most useful for those moving between various versions of BASIC, but for new programmers it may be of limited value.
  • Last Book of GW-Basic by the crusty and elusive Joseph Sixpack. Eccentric, entertaining, and sometimes educational.
  • BASIC Training: A GWBASIC tutorial by Steve Estvanik and converted to HTML by Thomas Antoni. This is thorough with the fundamentals of the language but only briefly touches graphics and sound.
